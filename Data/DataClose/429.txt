kind/bug severity/P1 status/open-for-dev target/branch team/platform status/in-progress 
Related to SPI branch only. After last changes, OpenShift infrastructure implementation workspaces don't start. The reason is that URL of websocket endpoint provided by Che is wrong. It should be <che-host-on-openshift>/wsmaster/websocket but now it is <che-host-on-openshift>:8080/wsmaster/websocket. Because of that bootstrapper of installers fails to connect to push statuses and logs on installers. Reproduction Steps: Start WS on OpenShift. It fails to start. OS and version: Diagnostics:
