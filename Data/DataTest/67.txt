Add support for APR in Tomcat 8
kind/question kind/enhancement kind/question 
Right now Che isn't using the performance benefits offered by Apache Portable Runtime (APR). These reduce the max latency during TLS negotiation dramatically and improve overall latency by at least two orders of magnitude. Most of the performance gain is due to native AES intrinsics and use of native Unix select(): https://tomcat.apache.org/tomcat-8.0-doc/apr.html http://linux.die.net/man/2/select
